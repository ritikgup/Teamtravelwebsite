UTTARAKHAND

A mystical land of mountains and mythologies, exquisite landscapes and exhilarating adventure, and wellness and yoga, Uttarakhand has something to offer every traveller. Popularly known as Devbhoomi, or the land of gods, the state is framed by the Himalayas and divided into two main regions, Garhwal and Kumaon. While Uttarakhand has several well-known destinations like Nainital, Mussoorie, Corbett National Park and Auli, and pilgrimage sites like Kedarnath, Badrinath, Rishikesh and Haridwar, explore the 13 lesser-known destinations from the state's 13 districts.

AULI

The Skiing Paradise of Garhwal | Uttarakhand Tourism

For skiing enthusiasts, January to March is the best time to visit. For nature lovers, October to March is the most appropriate time.

BADRINATH

Badrinath | Char Dham | Uttarakhand Tourism

The ideal time to visit Badrinath Temple is from May to October. The temple is closed from October/ November, and is reopened around April.

Mussoorie

Mussoorie - Queen of the Hills | Uttarakhand Tourism

The best time to visit Mussoorie is during summers, as it offers respite from the scorching summer heat. However, if you are looking for a secluded holiday, come here during winters to witness the snowfall.

Haridwar

Haridwar Tourism, Uttarakhand

The best time to visit Haridwar is from October to February when the weather is pleasant.
